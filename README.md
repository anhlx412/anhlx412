## 🧭 About Me

Visionary technology executive with **15+ years of engineering expertise** and **10+ years in senior leadership** — building and scaling engineering organizations, architecting enterprise-grade platforms, and driving end-to-end digital transformation across **EdTech, HealthTech, Mobility, and eCommerce** verticals.

I combine deep technical depth in **cloud-native, microservices, and AI-driven automation** with strong business acumen — translating technology strategy into measurable P&L outcomes.

- 🏗️ Built and led engineering departments of **30+ professionals** across Backend, Frontend, Data, DevOps, BA/PO & UI/UX — both in-house and outsourced
- ⚡ Stood up a fully operational engineering organization from **zero to production in 2 months** for a greenfield HealthTech / Telemedicine platform
- 🤖 Spearheaded **company-wide GenAI adoption**: LLM content recommendation, RAG knowledge bases, AI-driven student performance analytics
- ☁️ Directed enterprise-wide **AWS cloud migration** achieving **99.9% uptime SLA** with IaC (Terraform), Kubernetes, and GitOps workflows
- 🔗 Architected **integrated enterprise ecosystems** unifying LMS + CRM + HRM + ERP (SAP) into a centralized data backbone
- 📉 Reduced feature release lead time by **30%** through microservices architecture enabling independent team delivery cycles
- 🛡️ Established IT governance, cybersecurity policy & data protection compliance frameworks (PDPA, HIPAA-aligned)
- 💰 Owned **IT capex/opex planning**, achieving 20%+ annual cost efficiency through infrastructure consolidation and vendor renegotiation
- 🧠 Pioneered **AI-augmented engineering workflows** across the full delivery lifecycle — from requirements intake to production handoff — using AI Agents orchestrated on OpenAPI-spec-driven contracts: auto-generating API stubs, test cases, and documentation from specs, enabling junior and senior engineers to operate at equivalent output levels; reduced average task delivery time by **20–30%** while flattening skill-gap variance across the team

> *"Success is the sum of small efforts — repeated day in and day out."*
